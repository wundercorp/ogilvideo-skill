# Ogilvideo Production Blueprint

Builder Studio: https://builderstudio.dev

Use this blueprint to plan a premium motion video.

## Required deliverables

```text
video/brief.md
video/script.md
video/style-bible.md
video/storyboard.md
video/shot-list.md
video/ai-prompt-pack.md
video/asset-list.md
video/sound-map.md
video/qa-checklist.md
```

## Discovery questions

- Who is the viewer?
- What pain do they already understand?
- What pain do they need to feel in the first 2 seconds?
- What is the product promise?
- What proof can be shown without exaggeration?
- What action should the viewer take?
- What brand assets already exist?
- Which exact UI screens must appear?
- Which claims require legal or customer support?
- Which platforms and aspect ratios are needed?

## Asset checklist

### Brand

- Logo mark and wordmark.
- Typeface or fallback font.
- Color palette.
- Radius, shadow, stroke, spacing, and icon tokens.
- CTA language and URL.

### Product

- UI mockups.
- Product screenshots.
- Dashboard states.
- Tables, cards, forms, charts, and progress bars.
- Dummy data approved for public use.
- Cursor, pointer, tap, or voice-input states if needed.

### Visual

- Icons.
- Illustrations.
- 3D objects if needed.
- Maps if needed.
- Avatars or profile images.
- Background textures or gradients.

### Motion

- Transition motif.
- Camera rules.
- Easing curves.
- Motion presets.
- Number counter behavior.
- Loading/progress behavior.
- Logo resolve.

### Sound

- Music direction.
- UI clicks.
- Whooshes.
- Risers.
- Hits.
- Logo sound.

## Production phases

| Phase | Output | Review gate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy | brief, objective, core promise, proof | The story is worth making. |
| Copy | on-screen text, VO if any | Every line is short and supportable. |
| Style | style bible and keyframes | The look is on-brand and readable. |
| Storyboard | timeline and shot list | Every shot has a purpose. |
| Animatic | rough timing with still frames | Pacing works before polish. |
| Animation | motion passes and transitions | Motion supports the argument. |
| Compositing | UI, type, logo, depth, polish | No artifacts or illegible details. |
| Sound | music and effects | Sound supports timing and CTA. |
| Export | platform variants | Specs, safe area, and trim are correct. |

## Acceptance checklist

- Hook works without audio.
- Every shot has a purpose.
- Text is readable on a phone.
- UI looks real enough to trust.
- Motion motifs repeat with variation.
- Pacing has energy changes and holds.
- Claims are approved or marked placeholder.
- Logo and CTA remain visible long enough.
- Final export has no capture overlays.
- Audio leaves headroom and does not clip.
- Files are organized for future cutdowns.
